Sandwich

A light turkey sandwhich.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Turkey
  • Bread
  • Sliced Provolone Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Mayonnaise/li>

Instructions

  1. Slice your tomatoes and onions.
  2. Put Mayonnaise on your slices of bread.
  3. Build your sandwhich with all your ingredients.
