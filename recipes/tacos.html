Tacos

A simple ground beef taco.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Packet of Taco Seasoning
  • Taco Shells
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Iceberg Lettuce
  • Diced Onion

Instructions

  1. In a medium skillet brown the ground beef.
  2. Drain the fat from the ground beef.
  3. Add the taco seasoning according to the packet.
  4. Assemble your tacos.
